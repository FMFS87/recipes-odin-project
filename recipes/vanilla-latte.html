Vanilla Latte

Ingredients

  • 60ml of espresso
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla syrup
  • Whipped cream

instructions

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, combine milk and vanilla syrup. Stir frequently for about 4-5 minutes or until the milk has warmed up.
  2. Using an immersion blender or handheld mixer, beat milk until it foams up and has a frothy consistency.
  3. Pour espresso and the vanilla milk into a mug. Top with whipped cream and serve.
